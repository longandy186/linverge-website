Built for the Balkans

A local team with global standards, serving hotels across Belgrade and beyond.

"We believe hotel operations should be simpler, smarter, and more transparent."

Linverge was founded with a simple observation: hotels in the Balkan region spend too much time and money managing linen and supplies, often without knowing where their assets are or whether they're getting fair pricing. We set out to change that by combining RFID technology with integrated supply chain management, all delivered by a team that understands the local market.

Local Advantage

Why Being in Belgrade Matters

We're Where You Are

International solutions providers often treat the Balkans as an afterthought. We're based here, speak the language, understand the regulatory landscape, and can be at your hotel within hours, not days.

Our deep relationships with local laundries, logistics providers, and hotel associations mean we don't just deliver technology — we deliver a solution that actually works in this market.

Local Advantage

Why Belgrade Makes the Difference

Three reasons local hotels choose a local partner over international vendors

24h
On-Site Response

When something goes wrong at 7 AM before check-out, you can't wait 48 hours for a support ticket. Our Belgrade-based team can be at your hotel the same day. Hardware issues, software questions, training needs — handled in person, not over email.

Hub
Balkan Logistics Hub

Belgrade sits at the crossroads of Central Europe, the Mediterranean, and the Middle East. This geographic position gives us efficient access to supply chains from the EU, Turkey, and beyond — translating to faster delivery and better pricing for your hotel.

E-Fis
E-fiskalizacija Integration

Serbia's tax system requires electronic fiscalization (E-fiskalizacija). Our technical team has deep expertise in integrating with local billing and tax compliance systems. Your invoices, reports, and financial data flow seamlessly into local requirements — no workarounds needed.
